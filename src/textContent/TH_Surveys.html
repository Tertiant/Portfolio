I play Magic, and know firsthand the struggle of finding the cards I want at a reasonable price. However, my experience is not necessarily representative. To make sure that this website is useful to as broad an audience as possible, I surveyed a sample of Canadian Magic players. I found that:

  1. Comparing cards across shops takes time and effort. People feel that they don't have enough information about fair prices for their desired cards.
  2. People mostly buy Magic cards when available from their local game stores, but are willing to branch out to find better deals.
  3. People spend an average of 1.5 hours and $20-$100 buying Magic cards each month.
